Malaysia's Culture


In Southeast Asia, Malaysia is a diverse country with a mix of cultures and traditions. It's a beautiful blend of Malays, Chinese, Indians, and native groups. Malaysia is famous for various of festivals, languages, religions, buildings, and especially the yummy food which mixes various flavors and ingredients, showing its many cultures. It's a special and friendly mix that makes Malaysia unique and lively.
